Our Story and Vision

Established in 1949, Debagram Shibram Anandamayee Vidyapith has nurtured generations of students with a strong foundation in academics, values, and community spirit. Our Alumni Association was formed to honor this legacy, bridging the past and present by uniting former students under a common banner. We believe in fostering lifelong connections, giving back to our alma mater, and strengthening the bond between alumni, current students, and the broader school community.

About Our School

Situated in the heart of Debagram, Nadia, our school has been a beacon of education for over seven decades. With two major streams, Science and Humanities, along with vocational subjects under the NSQF Project, we have continuously provided high-quality education from Class IX to Class XII. Our state-of-the-art laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Geography, and Computer Science are designed to give students the practical knowledge and skills they need to excel in their academic and professional journeys.

Our Mission and Values

Our mission is to create a vibrant and engaged alumni network that supports the school, strengthens the bonds between former students, and empowers future generations. We are committed to:

  • Building lasting relationships among alumni across different batches.
  • Organizing events and reunions that celebrate our shared history and achievements.
  • Offering mentorship, career guidance, and networking opportunities for current students and young alumni.
  • Supporting school development initiatives that enhance the learning experience for future students.

Why Join the Alumni Association?

Being a member of our Alumni Association comes with numerous benefits:

  • Reconnect with Old Friends: Reunite with classmates, relive cherished memories, and celebrate milestones together.
  • Network with Fellow Alumni: Connect with professionals across diverse fields, opening doors to new career opportunities and collaborations.
  • Make a Difference: Contribute to initiatives that positively impact our school and community. Your support helps fund scholarships, infrastructure development, and student programs.
  • Stay Informed: Receive updates on school events, achievements, and opportunities to get involved.

Get Involved

We encourage every alumnus to take part in our efforts. Whether you want to volunteer your time, offer resources, or simply stay connected, there are countless ways to contribute. Together, we can continue to build a community that reflects the values and spirit of our school.
